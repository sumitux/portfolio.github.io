Angadee branding & design

Angadee is an e-commerce platform for 100% natural products. Also user can watch healthy recipe video and order ingredients. It has been developed for the Mobile App Ecosystem & web as well.

Challenges

Natural products are grown without the use of Chemicals and any other pesticides. The main challenge was to understand the benefits of natural products and find out how it will be beneficial for the customer.
The preference for natural products has been steadily increasing lately. In one survey, it showed that out 100 people, 87 preferred using natural products. So designing a product for a huge crowd was a big responsibility for me.
Hence, there were many challenges while going through the process...

  • Had to design a product that motivates the user to use natural products in day-to-day life
  • Branding should be very clear and intuitive

About Angadee

Angadee.io is an e-commerce platform for 100% natural products. It has been developed for the Mobile App Ecosystem & web as well.

The idea was to develop an app & website which will help users find chemical & pesticide-free products like food, cream, oil, juice, soap, shampoo, coffee, gel, powder, milk and more.

Research

Spending more time on the research phase gives more information, which can prevent costly mistakes.

I adopted the following three methods to understand user requirements.

User surveys

After approaching many users, I noted down a few points which helped us to understand their requirements. Here are some of their feedback.

  • Can I get this on a subscription basis?
  • Is there a way we could know more about the product?
  • What are the benefits of using a natural product
  • Will it be skin based
  • What is the side-effect of using natural products
  • What is the advantages & disadvantages
  • Higher or lower price compare to the product available in the market

However, 85% of digital shoppers like to buy natural products.

Market research

Although consumers are increasingly turning online to buy everything, only 3 in 10 Indians buy fresh produce, cream, coffee, oil, gel, powder, milk and more through e-commerce.

Market competition - Since there is not much competition in the market, this is the time to make something useful for the shopper.
One or two competitors are really doing well but they have not much engagement on the app/website.
