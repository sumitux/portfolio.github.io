Live video online classes - UX/UI

Live video online classes for engineering, medical & other examinations from India’s best teachers covering the toughest concepts & problems.

Overview

Had to design whole live classes experience

  • Users should be able to quickly find the Courses that are applicable to their selected exam
  • Though the Classes were offered for free, we wanted to ensure they have a premium feel
  • The design should encourage engagement during the class

Research

About HashLearn

HashLearn is an on-demand tutoring app. It connects students with top tutors from IIT’s, NIT’s, BIT’s - instantly, anytime, anywhere. Apart from that if a student is a Passport member(the equivalent of Amazon Prime), they will get unlimited access to everything - Practice, Tests, Videos and Doubt Clearing.

Problems understanding

We conducted a brainstorming session with users and tried to understand their problems & requirements. We asked multiple questions on the phone and wrote down everything about their requirements.
Everyone user is unique and has different requirements, these are the following problems & needs we listed down -

  • High-quality classes which are affordable
  • 100% completion of syllabus
  • Providing questions during and after the class for practice
  • Periodic tests

User story & there needs

After understanding their problems & requirements we decided to solve the most 3 valuable problems for our users.

  • Aman, a Class XII student, wants to be able to prepare for his JEE while not being able to spend lakhs on private tuition centres
  • Swaroop, wanted to practise questions during and after the test to ensure maximum retention
  • Rohit, a JEE aspirant, wanted periodic tests with an All-India ranking that will help track the progress of his preparation

User flow

On the basis of the user story & research, we created a user flow for each feature and developed the idea.

Use case - Student sign up and chooses an exam > We show them a list of courses that are applicable to their exam > They join a course > View all the past videos and schedule of future classes

Sketches & wireframing

After long research, we understood everything about users, their needs & requirements.
I started initial sketching and wireframing, we come up with many ideas and variations.

Final design

I used Figma to design all interfaces. HashLearn already had visual assets like color, typography, card, etc., I took 2 weeks to design all screens.

Conclusion

In just one month, we got over 12000 enrollments and attained our highest retention ever.
Our users responded positively to the new feature.
